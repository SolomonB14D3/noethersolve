# Behavioral Economics: Quantified Cognitive Bias Magnitudes

## Discovery Summary

Numerical measurement of cognitive biases using NoetherSolve decision theory tools.

## Key Findings

- **risky_investment**: 30% chance to gain $50K, 70% chance to lose $20K
- **job_offer_gamble**: Startup offer: 60% chance at $120K, 40% at $60K (current: $80K)
- **insurance_decision**: Pay $500 insurance premium (certain loss)
- **lottery_ticket**: Lottery: 1 in 100,000 chance at $10M, ticket costs $10
- **coin_flip_100_50**: 50-50: win $100 or lose $50
- **coin_flip_200_100**: 50-50: win $200 or lose $100
- **coin_flip_250_100**: 50-50: win $250 or lose $100 (should accept)
- **asymmetric_bet**: 70% win $1000, 30% lose $500
